How it works

Four states. Zero friction.

Walkie maps a full AI conversation onto the familiar mental model of a radio.

01
STANDBY

App is listening for your press. Radio-style indicators show you're on channel.

02
TRANSMIT

Hold the button. Speak. Live transcription appears as you talk.

03
PROCESS

Release to send. Your message goes to the AI provider of your choice.

04
RECEIVE

The AI's reply is read aloud. Conversation history updates automatically.
